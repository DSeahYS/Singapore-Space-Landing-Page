Our Journey

The Singapore Space Story

From a single optical satellite to a multi-modal constellation with sovereign SSA capabilities.

2015
TeLEOS-1 Launch
Singapore's first commercial Earth-observation satellite. 1m optical resolution in NEqO.
2022
DS-EO & NeuSAR
Multi-spectral optical and first fully polarimetric SAR microsatellite, designed by DSO.
2023
TeLEOS-2 & DS-SAR
PolSAR satellite with 1m SAR resolution and all-weather imaging capability. ~14 passes/day.
2025
STDP 2.0 Funding
S$60M top-up announced. Total STDP commitment exceeds S$200M for space innovation.
2026
SSA Partnerships
DSTA-Digantara and ST Engineering-LeoLabs collaborations for sovereign space traffic management.

Governance & Sustainability

Singapore is committed to responsible space use and sustainable orbital operations. Through SSA partnerships with Digantara and LeoLabs, and alignment with international space traffic management frameworks, Singapore ensures the long-term viability of the near-equatorial orbital environment.
